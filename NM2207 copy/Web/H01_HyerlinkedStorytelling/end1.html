RUN!

I should call for assistance before she attacks the other student. I made a lunge for the door handle but I was too late. I felt a pulsation of pain on my arm as she sunk her teeth in me. I pushed through the door agonizingly and called out weakly. I looked around - there was no one in sight.

"I need help, I need to get help," I whispered faintly.

Just then, I saw someone from the classroom opposite pointing at me frantically.
